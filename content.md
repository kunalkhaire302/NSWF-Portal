## PAGE: Events — Overview (`/events-1`)
This is a landing/dropdown page linking to 10 individual event write-ups (listed below in the order they appear in the nav menu).

### Event 1 — Awareness Campaign at Samata Vidyalaya (`/noise-pollution-awareness`)
- A noise-pollution awareness workshop was held at **Samata Vidyalaya, Uruli Devachi, Pune** (run by Sane Guruji Shikshan Sanstha) on **10 January 2026, 10:30–11:30 a.m.**
- Opened by **Rajendra Kulkarni** (Manager, NSWF), who spoke about the foundation's environmental work and the health effects of noise pollution, and introduced **Dr. Garima Kavathekar** (Founder & Director).
- A related student competition on noise pollution drew **100+ participants**; first/second/third prizes with gifts and certificates were awarded.
- Links to external coverage on stayfeatured.com.
- **Images:** event banner thumbnail + 2 event photos.

### Event 2 — Noise Pollution & E-Waste Awareness (`/noisepollutione-wasteawareness`)
- Held at **Gyanprabodhini Secondary and Junior College, Sasanenagar, Pune**, run by Mahatma Phule Vidya Niketan, in partnership with **Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd.**
- Timed around **Savitribai Phule Jayanti** and **National Girl Child Day**.
- Rajendra Kulkarni again addressed students, urging responsible e-waste disposal and noise reduction "in the coming year 2026."
- Links to external coverage on indiachronicle.in.
- **Images:** event thumbnail + 1 event photo.

### Event 3 — Real-Time Noise Measurement (`/real-timenoisemeasurement`)
- During **Ganesh Utsav 2025**, students from the **Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics (GIPE)** partnered with NSWF to measure festival noise levels at popular mandals, processions, and crowded areas across Pune using sound level meters.
- Purpose: gather real data on festival noise and raise awareness of its health impact on the elderly, children, and animals; engaged with mandal organizers to encourage eco-friendly celebrations.
- Links to external coverage on indiachronicle.in.
- **Images:** thumbnail + 4 event photos.

### Event 4 — Nutan Madhyamik Vidyalay, Vadki (`/nutanmadyamikvidyalayvadki`)
- Campaign held **20 September 2025** at Nutan Madhyamik Vidyalaya, Vadki, Pune.
- Used interactive sessions, visual presentations, and group discussions to teach students about sources/effects/prevention of noise pollution.
- Framed as a grassroots-level empowerment initiative for students.
- **Images:** thumbnail + 2 event photos.

### Event 5 — Dhyan Prabodhini School (`/dhyanprabodhinischool`)

### Event 6 — Vardhaman English Medium School (`/vardhamanenglishmediumschool`)
